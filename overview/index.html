FAME ME COMMUNITY FUND (FMCF)

As FARM ME commitment for some true value which contributes our realistic responsibility to the community, FMCF is established with a mission bringing the better value to the better lives.

The FMCF is used for Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR), environmental activities and other project related to social benefit.

Players completed all daily quests / weekly CSR tasks, all activities performed and other events on The Island of Angels. Total $ME token obtained which transfers to the fund called FMCF and the development board willing to be on behalf of all FARM Me players and support to go to the chosen charity project/ society/Government funds.

Fame token

$FAME TOKEN:

$FAME TOKEN

- Is the underlying cryptocurrency, playing a pivotal role throughout the game.

- Users can create their tangible assets on the blockchain.

- Total supply: 1,100,000,000

$FAME ROLE

Staking: Hold $FAME through the staking mechanism

Farming: Players can use $FAME tokens to participate in farming to provide liquidity for the project and receive rewards.

Payment: $FAME token is the Currency of the game used for transactions on the exchange, buying, and selling on the marketplace.

Governance: Players who own $FAME can vote on significant game development and operation decisions in Farm Me's, a decentralized organization (DAO).
